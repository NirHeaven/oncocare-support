隐私政策

引言

OncoCare（"我们"）高度重视您的隐私。本隐私政策说明我们如何保护您的个人健康信息。

数据存储

我们采用完全本地存储的方式：

  • 所有健康档案、检测记录、医疗影像等数据仅存储在您的设备本地
  • 无需注册或登录账号
  • 不会收集、上传或共享任何个人信息

数据安全

您的健康数据：

  • 完全由您个人控制
  • 不会与任何第三方共享
  • 不会上传到云端服务器
  • 可随时由您在App内清除

应用权限

OncoCare仅请求必要的本地存储权限，用于保存您的健康数据。不会请求通讯录、定位、相机等与健康管理无关的权限。

通知

应用可能会请求通知权限，用于提醒您检测、随访或健康管理计划。这些通知完全在本地生成，不会收集或传输任何数据。

第三方服务

我们使用硅基流动（SiliconFlow）平台的大模型API进行部分医学影像和报告的智能识别。相关隐私政策详见：https://docs.siliconflow.cn/cn/legals/privacy-policy

Privacy Policy

Introduction

OncoCare ("we") values your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we protect your personal health information.

Data Storage

We use completely local storage:

  • All health records, test results, and medical images are stored only on your device
  • No registration or login required
  • No personal information is collected, uploaded, or shared

Data Security

Your health data:

  • Is fully under your control
  • Is not shared with any third parties
  • Is not uploaded to any cloud server
  • Can be cleared by you at any time within the app

App Permissions

OncoCare only requests necessary local storage permissions to save your health data. It does not request contacts, location, camera, or other permissions unrelated to health management.

Notifications

The app may request notification permission to remind you about tests, follow-ups, or health management plans. These notifications are generated entirely locally and do not collect or transmit any data.

Third-Party Services

We use SiliconFlow's large model API for some medical image and report AI recognition. For privacy details, see: https://docs.siliconflow.cn/cn/legals/privacy-policy
